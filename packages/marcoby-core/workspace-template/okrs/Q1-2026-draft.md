# Marcoby OKRs — Draft (Q1 2026)

Notes
- Derived from The Marcoby Manifest. Targets are placeholders — adjust once baselined.
- Owners and exact metrics to be confirmed in first OKR review.

Company Objective O0: Establish Marcoby’s integrated operating foundation across Pulse, Catalyst, and Nexus
- KR0.1: Ship v1 cross-platform data model and event schema (Pulse↔Catalyst↔Nexus) by Mar 15
- KR0.2: Achieve 3 design partner pilots using at least 2 pillars (e.g., Pulse+Catalyst) by Mar 31
- KR0.3: Weekly integrated dashboard published (activation, retention, NRR, GM%) starting Feb 15

Pulse (VAR Marketplace)
Objective P1: Grow a credible, sticky catalog with seamless purchase-to-service handoff
- KR1.1: Publish 25 curated SKUs/bundles (SMB/Enterprise) with clear service attachments
- KR1.2: Implement AI recommender v0 (top-10 products) with >5% CTR on recommendations
- KR1.3: Loyalty beta live for 10 accounts; repeat purchase rate ≥30%
- KR1.4: Pulse→Catalyst auto-ticketing for 3 bundle types with <2 min handoff

Catalyst (Managed IT)
Objective C1: Make service delivery measurable and automated
- KR2.1: Central service dashboard live (SLA, MTTR, CSAT) covering 80% tickets
- KR2.2: Predictive maintenance playbook reduces repeat incidents by 20%
- KR2.3: Automated patch/backup workflows cover 90% managed endpoints
- KR2.4: CSAT ≥ 4.6/5 from pilot clients

Nexus (HQaaS)
Objective N1: Prove Nexus as the control plane for SMB/Enterprise ops
- KR3.1: Core modules (Tasks, Integrations, Insights) reach v1 with role-based access
- KR3.2: Public API (auth, webhooks, events) for 5 key integrations (M365, Slack/Teams, PSA, billing, Pulse)
- KR3.3: AI task copilot closes 25% of routine actions end-to-end in pilots

Operations & Strategy (OS)
Objective O&S1: Align execution and decisions with data
- KR4.1: Company KPI deck live weekly (Ops Review) with targets for each pillar
- KR4.2: Decision log process adopted; 10 material decisions captured with outcomes
- KR4.3: Platform integration runbook (people, process, tech) published and used

Marketing & Design (MD)
Objective M1: Clarify and launch the Marcoby story
- KR5.1: Rebrand v1 shipped (site, narrative, visual system) by Mar 1
- KR5.2: Launch 3 pillar campaigns; generate 50 qualified leads combined
- KR5.3: Content cadence: 8 blog posts, 6 shorts, 3 long-form pieces

Cloud Services (CS)
Objective CS1: Make Pulse hosting/domains truly turnkey
- KR6.1: End-to-end hosting automation (provisioning, SSL, DNS) with <10 min TTV
- KR6.2: Cloud management tools integrated into Nexus module
- KR6.3: 3 partner integrations signed and live

IT Solutions (IS)
Objective IS1: Reliability and security as default
- KR7.1: AI diagnostics reduce mean time to detect by 25%
- KR7.2: Security baseline (CIS-lite) applied to 100% managed clients
- KR7.3: Incident postmortem SOP adopted; 100% P1 incidents documented

Content & Media (CM)
Objective CM1: Educate the market on automation-first operations
- KR8.1: Launch educational series for Pulse/Catalyst/Nexus (min 9 episodes total)
- KR8.2: Resource library v1 with 20 artifacts (guides, templates, checklists)
- KR8.3: YouTube + social reach: 50k cumulative views; newsletter 1k subs

DevOps (MA)
Objective MA1: Ship faster with confidence
- KR9.1: CI/CD with blue/green or canary for core services; p95 deploy <15 min
- KR9.2: Error budgets and SLOs defined for 3 critical services; weekly review
- KR9.3: Automation coverage: 70% of repetitive ops tasks scripted
