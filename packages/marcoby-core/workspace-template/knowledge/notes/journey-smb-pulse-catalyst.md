# Customer Journey — SMB (Pulse→Catalyst)

ICP

- 20–200 employees; limited internal IT; M365-centric
- Pain: Sprawl of tools, reactive IT, security concerns

Stages & Plays

1. Awareness

- Trigger: Compliance scare or cost overrun
- Channel: Educational series + checklist lead magnet
- Asset: “SMB Modern IT bundle” landing page

2. Consideration

- Action: 30-min assessment + generated improvement plan
- Proof: Case study of 100-seat deployment; ROI one-pager

3. Purchase/Onboarding

- Commerce: Pulse bundle SKU (M365 hardening + MDM + backup)
- Hand-off: Auto-create Catalyst ticket + asset inventory via Nexus
- Setup: Provisioning runbook; TTV < 10 days

4. Adoption

- Plays: Monthly health score; automated patching; backup tests
- Integrations: M365, RMM/PSA
- Metric: Ticket volume -20% after 60 days; health score >80

5. Expansion

- Cross-sell: Security add-ons, endpoint upgrades, Nexus workflows
- Metric: NRR 115%; 2 add-ons within 90 days

6. Advocacy

- Program: Review/NPS + referral credit via Pulse loyalty
- Metric: NPS > 50; 1 referral per 5 customers

Events → Nexus

- order.created, entitlement.provisioned, ticket.closed, health.updated
